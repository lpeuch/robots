About

My Name is Lucile Peuch, I am 23 years old and apprentice in my working life in computer sciences and robotics.

I will be trying all along to present the bulk of services, army and industrial robots existing. I hope not to forget to much of them in the description. I don't expect to list every robots existing today and in the next years, it would be a Herculean task but I'll try my best.

This project is born after finding, somewhat surprising that there is no website listing all service robots (or I didn't find it...). I had to make research to find a robot adjustable to our project and spent more days looking over the Internet than I ever did! There was no list with every prices, size or little description of what was possible to imagine with a robot or even a name list...

I hope this list will be helpful to somebody. Don't hesitate to send me your feedback it would be really helpful to me.
